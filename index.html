The Artist

William Makori is a spoken word artist and poet born in 2007 in Kenya. His work is a powerful blend of raw emotion, rhythm, and unflinching truth— a voice that speaks directly to the heart of his generation.

From the bustling streets of Nairobi to stages across the country, William's performances are electric—moving crowds, challenging minds, and leaving audiences transformed. His poetry explores identity, resilience, hope, and the complex beauty of being young in Africa today.

"I write because silence is not an option. I perform because every word has the power to change something—someone."

— William Makori
